Main Plot

In Fura, a world of harmony, peace, and love, the Tree of Life stands as the heart of all creation. From this tree, eggs are born, bringing new life to the land. But within this paradise, there lie mysteries. The first egg, the Big Eagle, guarded secrets that have remained hidden for centuries. Legends speak of three great mysteries: the origins of the Tree of Life, the life of the Big Eagle, and the secrets of the sealed chilly mountain. One day, the sealed chilly mountain cracked, revealing a new enigma‚Äîa well that leads to somewhere dark. Meet Veta, a sheep unlike any other, with a love for cold weather. That's right! She truly loves the cold! Her journey for the ideal chilly spot will lead her to a place no one dares to go. She will discover the Land of Illusions, a realm of perpetual night, where the only inhabitants are the translucent animals. But beware, for without the cleansing mushrooms, one's mind may be lost to the shadows. Join Veta on an unforgettable journey as she unravels the secrets of this mystical land and learns the true meaning of courage, friendship, and the goodness everyone holds within.

The Land of Illusions

The Land of Illusions is a mystical, ethereal realm that exists parallel to Fura. It is a place of perpetual night, shrouded in cool mist and illuminated by a faint, otherworldly glow. The landscape is dotted with strange, bioluminescent plants and mushrooms, and the air is filled with the soft hum of unseen energies. This realm is inhabited by translucent creatures. The inhabitants of the Land of Illusions are unique, ethereal beings. These translucent animals resemble various creatures from Fura but are made of a delicate, almost ghostly substance. They emit a soft, radiant light and can phase through solid objects, making them seem like living spirits. These beings possess a deep connection to the natural energies of both worlds and have the ability to influence dreams and perceptions.

The Role of the Big Eagle

The Big Eagle, having discovered the well and the gateway to this otherworldly realm, understood the importance of keeping it hidden from those unprepared for its mysteries. The eagle encountered the translucent animals and learned of their purpose. Recognizing the potential dangers and the immense value of the knowledge within the Land of Illusions, the eagle chose to seal the well to protect both worlds.
